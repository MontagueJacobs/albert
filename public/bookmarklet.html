🌱 Sustainable Shopping

Sync your Albert Heijn purchases with one click

📚 Simple Setup (No extension needed!)

1

Drag this button to your bookmarks bar:

⬇️ DRAG THIS TO YOUR BOOKMARKS BAR ⬇️
🌱 Sync AH
💡 Tip: Don't see a bookmarks bar? Press Ctrl+Shift+B (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+B (Mac)
2

Go to ah.nl/producten/eerder-gekocht and log in with your AH account

3

Click the bookmarklet in your bookmarks bar. Done!

🔐 Privacy First

Your AH login credentials are NEVER stored or sent. The bookmarklet only reads the products already shown on the page. Your bonus card number is used to link your purchases to your account.

🎫 Personalized Bookmarklet (Optional)

If the bookmarklet can't find your bonus card automatically, enter it here to create a personalized version:

⬇️ YOUR PERSONALIZED BOOKMARKLET ⬇️
🌱 Sync AH

❓ How does it work?

The bookmarklet scans your "Previously Purchased" page for product names and prices. It automatically scrolls to load all products, then sends them to Sustainable Shopping where we analyze them for sustainability scores.
