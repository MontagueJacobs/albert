# Privacy Policy for Sustainable Shop - AH Sync Extension

**Last updated:** January 8, 2026

## Overview

The Sustainable Shop - AH Sync browser extension ("Extension") is designed to help users track the sustainability of their Albert Heijn grocery purchases. This privacy policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## Data We Collect

### 1. Purchase History from Albert Heijn
When you use the Extension on the Albert Heijn website, we collect:
- Product names
- Product prices
- Product URLs and IDs
- Product images (URLs only)

This data is only collected when you explicitly click the "Sync" button.

### 2. Account Information
If you sign in to the Extension:
- Email address (for authentication)
- Authentication tokens (stored locally in your browser)

### 3. Settings
- Your API server preference (stored locally)

## How We Use Your Data

- **Purchase data** is sent to our servers to calculate sustainability scores and provide personalized recommendations
- **Account information** is used solely for authentication purposes
- We do NOT sell your data to third parties
- We do NOT track your browsing activity outside of the explicit sync action

## Data Storage

- Authentication tokens are stored locally in your browser using the browser's secure storage API
- Purchase data is stored in our secure database (Supabase) and associated with your account
- You can delete your data at any time by contacting us

## Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties except:
- When required by law
- To provide the core service functionality (e.g., storing data in our database)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data
- Delete your data
- Export your data
- Opt out of data collection (by not using the sync feature)

## Security

We use industry-standard security measures including:
- HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
- Secure authentication via Supabase
- Row-level security ensuring users can only access their own data

## Contact

For privacy concerns or data requests, contact:
- Email: privacy@sustainable-shop.app
- GitHub: https://github.com/MontagueJacobs/albert

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date.
